Invoicinator ? What is that ?

INVOICINATOR is a an invoice maker browser-based app that let your quickly set-up a invoice for printing. It allows you to add item, calculate quote and even personalize your invoice. It is built using Javascript, JQUERY and HTML5

Finding an invoice maker is quite hard and expensive and tools available are quite complex to use. We realise that sometime all you need is to set up a quick invoice and that's it - INVOICINATOR is there now
